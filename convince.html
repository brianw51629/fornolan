Video on the negatives of Standardized Testing

Reasons to get rid of Standardized Tests

  • Leads to teachers "teaching to the test" rather than the material itself
  • Not authentic/performance based
  • Real Life is not multiple choice
  • Only one chance, student might have a bad day
  • Does not reflect true knowledge and ability
  • Leads to cheating
  • Cuts out fun to focus more on the tests
  • Damaging to kids mentally and emotionally
